SERVICES

The volunteers work together to Clean,

Repair, and Improve public spaces or other areas

that have been neglected, vandalized, or misused.

Rabuz

In the barangay of Pagatgat, Calabanga the rabuz activity is held once a week.

The bayanihan cleaning is open to everyone involved in this activity, including barangay leaders and youth.

This is to keep their immediate surroundings clean and orderly.

Tupad

Tupad is a similiar program to Rabuz. Workers in the Tupad program, on the other hand,

will receive a little sum of money to support them sustain their needs.

Cleanup Drive

Its barangay is also involved in a clean-up campaign.

In some portions of the rice field, the focus is mostly on cleaning the irrigation system.

This will assist farmers in having safer passage to their rice fields and better water flow.
